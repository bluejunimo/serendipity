About

This is an example of what it would look like when in use. The system is currently inactive.


Serendipity is a connected music system intended for design spaces of the future.

Nowadays, most students have their own (noise cancellation) headphones to listen to music. To improve community around music, weâ€™ve created a shared listening experience for future student design spaces.

Each device is placed in a separate area, perhaps in completely different design spaces. One person in each space activates their device by choosing the vibe of music they want to play. Once one user has submitted their vibe, it notifies the other devices to choose their vibe. The other user have 15 seconds to make a choice.

After the timer has run up, it randomly chooses a vibe based off of all the user's submitted vibes and plays a playlist of that type of music.
